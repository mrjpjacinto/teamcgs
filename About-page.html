Meet the Computer Science Interns at St. Paul University at San Miguel!

Hello, we are interns at St. Paul University at San Miguel, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. We are dedicated to learning and continually seeking to expand our knowledge.

We actively engage in coursework, collaborate on projects, and participate in activities that provide valuable hands-on experience.

Each of us has a unique story that led us to choose Computer Science as our field of study. Some of us were inspired by early exposure of technology and some of us don't really have knowledge about computer.

Together, our passion and curiosity drive us to continually learn and innovate. we look forward to the challenges and opportunities ahead and excited to make meaningful contributions to the projects we will be working on.
